How to do Diamond Paintings




How to Prepare for a Diamond Painting.

-Get a diamond painting kit.
-Prepare pen with wax by pressing it through the wax (after peeling back the plastic).
-Cut sealed diamond bags open and put the diamonds into little Ziploc bags or storage boxes, but keep track of the diamond numbers.
-Peel the plastic off the mat with the pattern on it.

How to Start a Diamond Painting.

-Use the pen to pick up a single diamond by pressing it on the raised side of the diamond.
-Place the diamond on the sticky mat on a spot that matches the color code.
-Repeat until all the labeled spots are covered.
-When you finish the painting, it is smart to put a sealant on it.
